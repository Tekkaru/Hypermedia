Heartweaver

Details

Project Description

Heartweaver was my first "pro" project in a company and a very ambitious one, we had a good team to develop the game but with time, Ace lose his father in a car crash and she starts writing in her diary provoking to start the game in the inner world of Ace fighting this lose duel, Mark will follow and guide Ace, he is going to be his diary.

Time Developing

The project was developed with 2 years and 6 months, since the pre-productions and design till the implementation and release.

Project Role

I started as a Junior Game Designer in practice the first months but at the ended I ascended the company and evolved to the unique Level Designer (as Junior) and ending as a Lead Level Designer in the project.

Key Characteristics

  • Unique worlds and narrative
  • Incredible graphics and art style
  • Puzzle System
  • Rewards for the more explorers one
